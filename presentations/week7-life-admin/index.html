Life Admin Setup

The stuff you MUST sort before you leave school

Deadline: December 2026
Before you finish Year 12, get your adult life sorted

Why This Matters NOW

Once you leave school, no one reminds you

  • No teacher checking you've submitted forms
  • No parent making sure you've done the paperwork
  • No school admin chasing you up
  • You're on your own

Sort it now while you have support, structure, and time. Don't wait until you need it urgently.

myGov Account

Your gateway to government services

  • Create it before December!
  • Links to: Tax Office, Centrelink, Medicare, Child Support
  • You'll need it for tax returns, healthcare, study payments
  • Use a proper email address you'll keep forever
  • Set up two-factor authentication immediately

Action: Go to my.gov.au → Create account → Link your Medicare and ATO

Tax File Number (TFN)

Essential for ANY job

  • Takes up to 28 days to arrive
  • You CANNOT start a job properly without it
  • Without it, you get taxed at the highest rate (47%!)
  • Apply through myGov or ATO website
  • Keep it secure - it's like your financial ID

Timeline: Apply in September → Receive in October → Ready for December holiday jobs

Medicare Card in Your Own Name

Not just on parents' card anymore

  • You can get your own card from age 15
  • Essential for doctor visits, prescriptions, hospitals
  • Needed for university health services
  • Can be used as ID for some services
  • Apply through myGov → Medicare section

Why separate? Privacy, independence, and you'll need it when you move out or travel

Bank Account

Time to be financially independent

  • Open an account in YOUR name only
  • Get a debit card for online purchases
  • Set up internet banking and the mobile app
  • Learn to check your balance and transactions
  • Consider a savings account for goals

Pro tip: Most banks offer fee-free accounts for students. Compare features before choosing.

USI (Unique Student Identifier)

Needed for ANY training or uni

  • Required for TAFE, university, apprenticeships, traineeships
  • Links all your qualifications together forever
  • Free to create, takes 5 minutes
  • You'll need ID documents (passport, driver's licence, etc.)
  • Create it at usi.gov.au

Important: Many institutions won't enrol you without it. Don't wait until enrolment day!

Driver's Licence or Proof of Age Card

Your primary ID

  • Most common form of photo ID in Australia
  • Needed for: Jobs, bank accounts, signing leases, travel
  • If not driving, get a Proof of Age card from Transport WA
  • Keep it current - check expiry dates
  • Learn where to keep it safe

Reality check: You'll be asked for photo ID constantly. Have it ready.

Professional Email Address

firstname.lastname@gmail.com
NOT coolkid99@hotmail.com

  • Create a professional email for adult life
  • Format: firstname.lastname@ or initial.lastname@
  • Use it for: Job applications, university, government services
  • Keep your fun email separate for friends and shopping
  • Check it daily - important stuff comes via email

First impression: david.smith@gmail.com gets the job. sk8rboi2008@hotmail.com doesn't.

Phone Plan in Your Own Name

Leave the family plan

  • Time to get your own mobile plan
  • Builds credit history when you pay bills on time
  • Prepaid or monthly plan - you choose
  • Learn to manage data and costs
  • Keep the same number - just change account holder

Adult skill: Managing a phone bill is practice for managing all your bills
