Copyright Matters

Who Owns AI-Generated Images?

AI images are created by training on millions of existing images from the internet ‚Äî often without permission from original artists.

The legal status is unclear. Courts are still deciding whether AI art infringes copyright.

‚öñÔ∏è

Current Legal Position

In Australia, copyright may not protect AI-generated works without substantial human creativity. Commercial use is risky.

üéì

For School Projects

Generally OK for educational purposes, but always disclose when you use AI-generated content.

Appropriate vs Inappropriate Use

‚úì Appropriate Uses
  • Placeholder images for mockups and drafts
  • Concept exploration and brainstorming
  • Educational projects where AI use is disclosed
  • Generating references for traditional art
  • Personal creative experiments
  • When you lack photography/design budget
‚úó Inappropriate Uses
  • Pretending AI art is your own original work
  • Replacing human artists when you have budget
  • Creating deepfakes or misleading images
  • Generating images of real people without consent
  • Commercial use without checking terms of service
  • Assignments that explicitly require original work

Think About the Impact

üë®‚Äçüé®

Impact on Artists

Many artists feel their work was used without permission to train AI models. Consider supporting human creators when possible.

üåç

Bias & Representation

AI models can perpetuate stereotypes and biases from their training data. Be critical of what they generate.

üîç

Misinformation Risk

Photorealistic AI images can be used to deceive. Always be transparent when sharing AI-generated content.

üí∞

Economic Impact

AI tools are displacing some creative jobs. Think about how your use affects professional creatives.

üìù

Getting Better Results

üéØ

Be Specific with Prompts

Include details about style, lighting, composition, colours, and mood. "A beach" vs "a serene beach at golden hour"

üîÑ

Iterate and Refine

Generate multiple versions. Use the one closest to your vision and refine from there.

üìê

Choose the Right Aspect Ratio

Square for Instagram, 16:9 for presentations, 9:16 for stories. Pick before generating.

üé®

Reference Artistic Styles

"In the style of..." or mention art movements: impressionist, minimalist, cyberpunk, etc.

üö´

Use Negative Prompts

Many tools let you specify what NOT to include: "no text, no watermarks, no people"
