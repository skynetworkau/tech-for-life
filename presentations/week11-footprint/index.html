Two Types of Footprint

Active Footprint

Things you deliberately post and share:

  • Social media posts
  • Comments and replies
  • Photos and videos
  • Profile information
  • Reviews you leave

Passive Footprint

Data collected without you actively sharing:

  • Browsing history & cookies
  • Location data
  • App usage patterns
  • Purchase history
  • Photos others tag you in
