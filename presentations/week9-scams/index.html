They Pretend to Be Real

üì±
Optus / Telstra Scams
"Your account has been suspended" or "Urgent bill payment required." Uses fake sender IDs that look like real telco numbers. Links to fake payment pages.
üí∞
ATO Tax Scams
"You owe back taxes" or "Tax refund available." Threatens legal action or arrest. The real ATO never demands immediate payment by gift card or crypto.
üì¶
Australia Post Scams
"Parcel waiting for delivery, pay $2.50 fee." Fake tracking links. Real AusPost uses mypost.com.au domain, not variations.
üè¶
Bank Impersonation
Commonwealth, NAB, ANZ, Westpac logos in fake emails. "Verify your identity" or "Suspicious activity detected." Banks never ask for passwords via email or SMS.
