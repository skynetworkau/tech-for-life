Medicare Levy Surcharge

$93,000

If you earn over this amount and DON'T have private hospital cover, you pay 1-1.5% extra tax

Example: Earning $100,000/year without hospital cover = $1,000-$1,500 extra tax per year.

Basic hospital cover often costs less than the surcharge, so it's financially smarter to get covered.

Not relevant yet, but will be when you're earning well!

Lifetime Health Cover Loading

Before Age 31
No loading ‚Äî Get hospital cover at base price
After Age 31
2% loading per year ‚Äî Wait until 35? Pay 8% extra. Wait until 40? Pay 18% extra.

Car Insurance for Young Drivers

Why It's Expensive

Premiums are HIGH for under-25s because statistics show more accidents in this age group.

Ways to Reduce:

  • ‚Ä¢ Named driver on parents' policy
  • ‚Ä¢ Build no-claim bonuses
  • ‚Ä¢ Choose higher excess
  • ‚Ä¢ Less powerful car
What's an Excess?
The amount you pay first when making a claim. Higher excess = lower premium. Typical excess: $500-$1,000.
What Affects Premiums
‚Ä¢ Age (under 25 = higher)
‚Ä¢ Car type (sports car = higher)
‚Ä¢ Suburb (high crime = higher)
‚Ä¢ Driving history (accidents = higher)

Income Protection

What It Does

Pays you if you can't work due to illness or injury.

Usually covers 75% of your income for a set period (3 months to 2 years, depending on policy).

Often included in your superannuation ‚Äî check your super statements!

When It Matters
More important once you have regular bills to pay ‚Äî rent, car payments, loans. Less critical when living at home with no major expenses.
Check Your Super
Most super funds include default income protection and life insurance. Log into your super account to see what you already have.
