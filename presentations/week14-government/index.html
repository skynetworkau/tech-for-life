AI & The Future

Why public service might be the smart long-term play

üõ°Ô∏è Why Government is Safer

  • Complex human judgment roles
  • Policy & democratic accountability
  • Face-to-face citizen services
  • Security clearance requirements
  • Union protections & job security
  • New AI oversight roles emerging

‚ö†Ô∏è Roles at Higher Risk

  • Data entry & processing
  • Basic admin tasks
  • Routine financial processing

  • Simple customer queries
