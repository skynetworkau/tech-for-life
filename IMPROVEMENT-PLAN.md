### 3. Content Gaps - Week 8 (Money & Tax)

#### Accountant Benefits - MISSING
Add to tax section:
- Lodge until March (vs October deadline for self-lodgers)
- Bigger returns - know all deductions
- Less audit risk - professional preparation
- Help if audited - representation
- Cost: $100-300 but usually pays for itself
- First tax return should use accountant to learn

### 4. Content Gaps - Week 12 (Trades & Apprenticeships)

#### RTOs Missing
Add:
- **RTOs (Registered Training Organisations)**: Alternative to TAFE
- Private RTOs often faster, more flexible
- Examples in WA: SMYL, MPA Skills, PEER
- Group Training Organisations (GTOs): Employ apprentices, place with host employers
- Can do trade qualification without going to TAFE at all

### 5. Content Gaps - Week 14 (Business)

#### Payment & Invoicing Systems - MISSING
Add:
- **Stripe**: Online payments, invoicing, subscriptions
- **Square**: Point of sale, invoicing, appointments
- **PayPal Business**: Invoicing, payments
- **Xero**: Accounting + invoicing built-in
- **MYOB**: Australian accounting + invoicing
- **Invoice Ninja**: Free invoicing
- **Wave**: Free accounting + invoicing
- Benefits: Professional invoices, automatic reminders, payment tracking, tax reporting
